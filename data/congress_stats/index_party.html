Overall Personality Traits: Differences Between Democrats and Republicans

Overall, Democrats and Republicans scored similarily on personality traits, and there are more differences within groups than between them. However, there were some differences between groups:

  • In general, Democrats exhibited more negative traits on their twitter feeds. We hypthesize that this may be due to being the 'out of power' part
